## Course Goals

By the end of this course, learners will:

1. **Understand** how quantum computers fundamentally differ from classical computers
2. **Grasp** core quantum principles that enable quantum advantage
3. **Learn** how quantum algorithms work conceptually
4. **Be able to** write and simulate simple quantum circuits
5. **Understand** real-world applications and current limitations

## Prerequisites

- Basic algebra and linear algebra concepts
- Understanding of classical computing fundamentals
- Programming experience (any language) recommended
- Familiarity with complex numbers helpful but not required
